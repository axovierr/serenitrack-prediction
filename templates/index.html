About Us

What is SereniTrack?

A smart tool designed to help students better understand their mental wellbeing

What is it?

SereniTrack is a web platform that helps students detect depression symptoms simply and accurately through a data-driven questionnaire.

Why SereniTrack?

We help increase self-awareness and provide a meaningful first step toward maintaining students' mental health and overall wellbeing.

How does it work?

It processes personal data from users to provide depression predictions based on trained machine learning datasets from real student data.
